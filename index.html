Program Details
THE PLAN
Program Overview
Schedule
4 days/week — Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri or Mon/Wed/Fri/Sat
Duration
45–55 minutes per session
Structure
A/B split × 2 — rotate Week A and Week B
Equipment
2×40 lb to start; upgrade swings/deadlifts to 53–62 lb as you adapt
Session Rules
  • Open every session with 5 min movement prep — joint circles, hip hinges, goblet squat holds
  • Close every session with 3 min mobility — hip flexor stretch, thoracic rotation, dead bug
  • Rest 60–90 s between sets unless noted otherwise
  • RPE targets: strength work 7–8/10 · conditioning finishers 8–9/10
Progression Map
Wks 1–3
Technique & baseline — use 40 lb, rest fully, dial in form
Wks 4–6
Volume load — add 1 set to each main lift; shorten rest 10 s
Wks 7–9
Intensity bump — upgrade swings & deadlifts to 53–62 lb bell
Wk 10
Deload — cut volume by 40%, same movements, flush fatigue
Wks 11+
Repeat with heavier load across all lifts or add complexity
Equipment Upgrades
→
53 lb (24 kg) bell — next swing/deadlift progression
→
62 lb (28 kg) bell — double front squat, clean & press progression
→
Adjustable KB or rack of 3 bells gives maximal variety
Note for tall athletes: At 6′6″, pay extra attention to hip hinge depth on deadlifts and swings, rack position on cleans, and overhead stability on presses. Prioritize controlled eccentric on all squats. If any bilateral move causes discomfort, substitute the unilateral variation at the same load.
